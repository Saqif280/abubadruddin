Ralph Standbrook Scholarship (only for GIS major)

  • Application deadline: May 1st of the year
  • Requirement: Candidate must have completed at least 6 credits of GIS courses with a GPA of 3.0 or better
  • Who can apply: A returning sophomore majoring in GIS
  • Where to apply: The CCC Foundation
